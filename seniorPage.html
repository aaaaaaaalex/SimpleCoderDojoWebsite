Coding with CIT-Dojo as a Senior

Teenagers in the Seniors' Club will be introduced to programming with Python and Unity 3D, two powerful tools that are used extensively worldwide.

Python, a scripting language like PHP and Perl, can be used in Web Development but can also be used to make applications, such as the popular 3D-Modelling engine Blender

Unity 3D, on the other hand delves further into the world of 3D computer gaming. Rather than being it's own programming language, it is a 3D program designed for making and learning about 3D games and object-oriented scripts. To do so , it utilizes the power of other scripting languages such a JavaScript, C-Sharpe and Boo

Dates and Times

CIT-Dojo hosts workshops for ages 7 to 17 every Friday , from 4pm to 6pm.

Our Senior students also have an opportunity to enter their own creative projects in the Coolest-Projects competition hosted every year in Dublin, Ireland. This competition takes thousands of entries but in June of 2015, our very own member Cian Sheehan won first prize for his revolutionary High-Tech shower that is controlled via his smartphone!
