Welcome To The Coder-Dojo Homepage!

About: CoderDojo is a world-wide organization established with the aim of letting the younger generation experience and learn about technology, and it's technical background of programming languages, apps, web-pages, etc. Although CoderDojo was initially founded in Dublin, Ireland, it now streches over many countries and continents across the world. CIT CoderDojo is available to mentor all those aged from 7years old to 16. Children aged 7 to 11 can apply for the Juniors' Club, while teenagers from to 12 to 16 can apply for the Seniors' Club

“Seeing children coming up with novel creative concepts for their projects. Coming up with these ideas brings out their unique imaginative qualities. It gives them the courage to be the colourful individual the world wants them to be.”
-Ursula Clarke Everett (Docklands Dojo, Dublin)
